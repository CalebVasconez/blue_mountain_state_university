History of Blue Mountain State

Blue Mountain State University was first established in the year 1838 by a scholar named Alex Moran. It is located in Erie, Pennsylvania. With an in-state tuition of $8,219 and out of state tuition of $21,583. BMSU is proud to provide scholarships based on academic and athletic achievement in high school to help with tuition costs.


Blue Mountain State University competes in the West Virginia Intercollegiate Athletic Conference where they have achieved multiple conference titles as well as a handful of National Championships. The basketball team is coming of a record season winning the conference as well as advancing all the way until the Final Four. The football team has won 3 of the last four National Championships and are looking for the three-peat this season. The women's teams have been successful as well. Volleyball and basketball have not had a losing season since 2003 and are continuing to get better each year.
