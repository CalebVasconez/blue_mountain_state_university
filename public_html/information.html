History of Blue Mountain State

How The School Started

Blue Mountain State University was first established in the year 1838 by a scholar named Alex Moran.

Location and Tution

It is located in Erie, Pennsylvania. With an in-state tution of $8,219 and out of state tuition of $21,583. That number can fluxuate depending on scholarships and your academic achievements in high school.


Virginia Intercolegiate Athletic Conference

Blue Mountain State University competes in the West Virginia Intercollegiate Athletic Conference where they have acheived multiple conference titles as well as a handful of National Champioships. The basketball team is coming of a record season winning the conference as well as advancing all the way until the Final Four. The football team has won 3 of the last four National Championships and are looking for the three-peat this season. The womens teams have been succesful as well. Volleyball and basketball have not had a losing season since 2003 and are continuing to get better each year.

Track and Field

The Track and Field team for BMSU has also had multiple conference trophies. They have had at least one member from the team go on to compete in Nationals every year.They are on a winning conference streak of 4 wins and they plan to get their 5th trophy this year.
